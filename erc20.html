About

ERC Minter is a open source website for minting ERC20 tokens on the Ethereum and Polygon blockchain. ERC Minter does not charge any service fees when minting ERC20 tokens. If you want to support the project please donate via the Ethereum and Polygon addresses stated in the Donations section.
